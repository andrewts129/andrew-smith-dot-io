Who Were The Biggest One Hit Wonders? (WIP)

Defining a "big" one hit wonder as an artist who had one big hit, and very little success otherwise.

For every artist that has appeared in the Billboard Top 40 at least once, but less than three times, a "One-Hit-Wonderness" score was calculated. Using Last.fm to get the total number of plays for an artist's top ten songs, their score was calculated by the following ratio:

(standard deviation of plays including top song) / (standard deviation of plays excluding top song)

All artists with a score above 20 are ranked below. Included is the title of their top hit, as well as the year it was released.
